# ProtoFlux:TimeSpanMinutes

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:TimeSpanMinutes

Minutes

TimeSpan

\*

Timespan

The `TimeSpan Minutes` node takes in a [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan") and returns with only the amount of minutes in that TimeSpan. Anything other than the minutes will be ignored.

![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/c/c1/SuggestionIcon.svg)

If you want the accumulated minutes that includes the fractional portion of a minute in one value, use [TimeSpan Total Minutes](https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:TimeSpan_Total_Minutes "ProtoFlux:TimeSpan Total Minutes") instead.


## Inputs

### TimeSpan ( [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan"))

The TimeSpan we are getting info from.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [int](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Int "Type:Int"))

Returns the amount of minutes in this TimeSpan as an int.
