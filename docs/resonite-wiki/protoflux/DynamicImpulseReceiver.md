The **Dynamic Impulse Receiver** node is the recipient of a [dynamic impulse](https://wiki.resonite.com/Dynamic_impulse "Dynamic impulse"). Its trigger analogue is the [Dynamic Impulse Trigger](https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:Dynamic_Impulse_Trigger "ProtoFlux:Dynamic Impulse Trigger") node.

## Outputs

### OnTriggered ( [Call](https://wiki.resonite.com/Impulses "Impulses"))

Sends an [impulse](https://wiki.resonite.com/Impulse "Impulse") when the node has received a dynamic impulse with the specified `Tag`.

## Globals

### Tag ( [String](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:String "Type:String"))

The tag to listen for impulses on.
