# ProtoFlux:E

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:E

e

Constants

Called **[Euler's number](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E_(mathematical_constant) "wikipedia:E (mathematical constant)")**, e is approximately equal to 2.71828.

It is the base of natural logarithms.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [float](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Float "Type:Float"))

Returns the mathematical constant.

## Examples

This node is useful for the following reasons when creating systems:

- Exponential Growth and Decay
- Random Events and Probabilities
- Economics and Resource Management (such as [Interest and Inflation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Compound_interest))
- Difficulty Scaling
- AI Behavior
- Smooth Curves
