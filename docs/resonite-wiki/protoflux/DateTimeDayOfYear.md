# ProtoFlux:DateTimeDayOfYear

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:DateTimeDayOfYear

Day of Year

DateTime

\*

DateTime

The `DateTime Day Of Year` node takes in a [DateTime](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:DateTime "Type:DateTime") and returns the day of the year (represented as an [int](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Int "Type:Int")) from that DateTime. This takes into account the days in the months accumulated so far in that year and adds them together, as well as any [leap year](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leap_year) days that were in that year.

## Inputs

### DateTime ( [DateTime](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:DateTime "Type:DateTime"))

The DateTime we are getting info from.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [int](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Int "Type:Int"))

The day of the year from this DateTime.
