Spherical Harmonics

The **Pack SH1** node takes in values and returns the [SphericalHarmonicsL1](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:SphericalHarmonicsL1 "Type:SphericalHarmonicsL1") type.

## Inputs

### SH0 (Pseudo-Generic)

A part of a spherical harmonic value.

### SH1 (Pseudo-Generic)

A part of a spherical harmonic value.

### SH2 (Pseudo-Generic)

A part of a spherical harmonic value.

### SH3 (Pseudo-Generic)

A part of a spherical harmonic value.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [SphericalHarmonicsL1](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:SphericalHarmonicsL1 "Type:SphericalHarmonicsL1"))

The L1 Spherical Harmonics.
