From Unix Seconds
UnixSeconds
*
IsLocal
Time
The `From Unix Seconds` node takes in the Unix seconds and then converts it into a [DateTime](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:DateTime "Type:DateTime"), along with an option to offset it to make it a local DateTime (based on your time zone). The amount of seconds given will count up from the [Unix epoch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Unix_time). 
## Inputs
### UnixSeconds ([long](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Long "Type:Long"))
The total Unix seconds for converting into a DateTime. 
### IsLocal ([bool](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Bool "Type:Bool"))
Should adjust for local time. 
## Outputs
### * ([DateTime](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:DateTime "Type:DateTime"))
Returns the DateTime from the Unix seconds.
