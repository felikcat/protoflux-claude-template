# ProtoFlux:DeltaAngleRad

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:DeltaAngleRad

Delta Angle Rad

From

\*

To

Vectors

The **Delta Angle Rad** node takes in 2 [float2](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Float2 "Type:Float2") or [double2](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Double2 "Type:Double2") vectors and returns the radians of those two vectors.

![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/c/c1/SuggestionIcon.svg)

If you need to determine the degrees from 2 vectors, use [Delta Angle](https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:Delta_Angle "ProtoFlux:Delta Angle") instead.


## Inputs

### From (Pseudo-Generic)

The first vector.

### To (Pseudo-Generic)

The second vector.

## Outputs

### \\* (Pseudo-Generic)

Returns the angle of these 2 vectors in radians.
