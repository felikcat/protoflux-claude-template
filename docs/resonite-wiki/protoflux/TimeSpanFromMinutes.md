# ProtoFlux:TimeSpanFromMinutes

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:TimeSpanFromMinutes

Time Span From Minutes

Value

\*

Timespan

The `TimeSpan From Minutes` node takes in the amount of minutes and converts it into a [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan") that shows the span of time in minutes.

## Inputs

### Value ( [double](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Double "Type:Double"))

The amount of minutes.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan"))

Returns the TimeSpan with the amount of minutes.
