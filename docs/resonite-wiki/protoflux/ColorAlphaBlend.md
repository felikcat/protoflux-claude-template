# ProtoFlux:ColorAlphaBlend

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:ColorAlphaBlend

\*

Destination

Colors

Color Alpha Blend does an [alpha blend](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alpha_compositing) of its two input colors. The source is blended "onto" the destination, and the higher the source alpha, the more of the "source" color and less of the "destination" color comes through.

Alpha values are summed and clamped to a maximum of 1.

## Inputs

### Source ( [Color](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Color "Type:Color"))

The color that is blended "onto" the destination.

### Destination ( [Color](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Color "Type:Color"))

The destination color that the source is blended "onto".

## Outputs

### \\* ( [Color](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Color "Type:Color"))

The blended color result.
