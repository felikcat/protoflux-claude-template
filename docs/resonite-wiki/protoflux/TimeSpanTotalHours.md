# ProtoFlux:TimeSpanTotalHours

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:TimeSpanTotalHours

Hours

TimeSpan

\*

Timespan

The `TimeSpan Total Hours` node takes in a [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan") and returns with the amount of hours in that TimeSpan, including the fractional portion of a hour, as well as anything longer than an hour will be added into the total.

## Inputs

### TimeSpan ( [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan"))

The TimeSpan we are getting info from.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [double](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Double "Type:Double"))

Returns the amount of hours in this TimeSpan as an double.
