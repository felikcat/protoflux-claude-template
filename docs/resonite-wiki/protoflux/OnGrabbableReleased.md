On Grabbable Released is a ProtoFlux node that monitors an [IGrabbable](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:IGrabbable "Type:IGrabbable") for when something or someone lets go of it and sends an [Impulse](https://wiki.resonite.com/Impulses "Impulses").

## Outputs

### OnGrabbed ( [Call](https://wiki.resonite.com/Impulses "Impulses"))

Sends an impulse when someone or something lets go of Grabbable ( [IGrabbable](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:IGrabbable "Type:IGrabbable")).

## Globals

### Grabbable ( [IGrabbable](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:IGrabbable "Type:IGrabbable"))

The [IGrabbable](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:IGrabbable "Type:IGrabbable") to monitor for when something or someone lets go of it.
