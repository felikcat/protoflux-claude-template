# ProtoFlux:TimeSpanTotalMinutes

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:TimeSpanTotalMinutes

Minutes

TimeSpan

\*

Timespan

The `TimeSpan Total Minutes` node takes in a [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan") and returns with the amount of minutes in that TimeSpan, including the fractional portion of a minute, as well as anything longer than a minute will be added into the total.

## Inputs

### TimeSpan ( [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan"))

The TimeSpan we are getting info from.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [double](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Double "Type:Double"))

Returns the amount of minutes in this TimeSpan as an double.
