Lerp
Rotation
The **Lerp** node takes in a starting rotation and an ending rotation as a [quaternion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quaternion) value along with a lerped value to control the journy between those two rotations, and returns a Quaternion that is currently at that lerped value. 
## Inputs
### From (Pseudo-Generic)
The starting point of this rotation. 
### To (Pseudo-Generic)
The destination of the rotation. 
### Lerp ([float](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Float "Type:Float"))
The amount to rotate by over the interpolation. 
## Outputs
### * (Pseudo-Generic)
Returns a quaternion that has been rotated by the lerp value. 
## See Also
  * Wikipedia's definition of [quaternion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quaternion) & [euler angles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euler_angles).
