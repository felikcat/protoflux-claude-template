DateTime

The `DateTime Day` node takes in a [DateTime](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:DateTime "Type:DateTime") and returns the day of that DateTime.

## Inputs

### DateTime ( [DateTime](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:DateTime "Type:DateTime"))

The DateTime we are getting info from.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [int](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Int "Type:Int"))

The day portion of the DateTime.
