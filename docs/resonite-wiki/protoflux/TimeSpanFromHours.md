# ProtoFlux:TimeSpanFromHours

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:TimeSpanFromHours

Time Span From Hours

Value

\*

Timespan

The `TimeSpan From Hours` node takes in the amount of hours and converts it into a [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan") that shows the span of time in hours.

## Inputs

### Value ( [double](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Double "Type:Double"))

The amount of hours.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan"))

Returns the TimeSpan with the amount of hours.
