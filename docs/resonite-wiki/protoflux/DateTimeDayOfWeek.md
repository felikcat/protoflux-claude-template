# ProtoFlux:DateTimeDayOfWeek

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:DateTimeDayOfWeek

Day of Week

DateTime

\*

DateTime

The `DateTime Day Of Week` node takes in a [DateTime](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:DateTime "Type:DateTime") and returns the day of the week for that DateTime.

## Inputs

### DateTime ( [DateTime](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:DateTime "Type:DateTime"))

The DateTime we are getting info from.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [DayOfWeek](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:DayOfWeek "Type:DayOfWeek"))

The day of the week of this DateTime.
