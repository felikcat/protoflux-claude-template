> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:ValueMin

The **Value Min** node takes in two values and returns the lower of the two values.

## Inputs

### A (Pseudo-Generic)

The first value.

### B (Pseudo-Generic)

The second value.

## Outputs

### \\* (Pseudo-Generic)

Returns the lower of the two values.
