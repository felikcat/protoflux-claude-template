# ProtoFlux:TimeSpanTotalMilliseconds

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:TimeSpanTotalMilliseconds

Milliseconds

TimeSpan

\*

Timespan

The `TimeSpan Total Milliseconds` node takes in a [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan") and returns with the amount of milliseconds in that TimeSpan, including the fractional portion of a millisecond, as well as anything longer than a millisecond will be added into the total.

## Inputs

### TimeSpan ( [TimeSpan](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:TimeSpan "Type:TimeSpan"))

The TimeSpan we are getting info from.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [double](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Double "Type:Double"))

Returns the amount of milliseconds in this TimeSpan as an double.
