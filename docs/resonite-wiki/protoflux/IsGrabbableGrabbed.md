# ProtoFlux:IsGrabbableGrabbed

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/ProtoFlux:IsGrabbableGrabbed

Is Grabbable Grabbed

Grabbable

\*

Grabbable

Is Grabbable Grabbed is a ProtoFlux node that gets whether or not an [IGrabbable](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:IGrabbable "Type:IGrabbable") is being grabbed by someone or something.

## Inputs

### Grabbable ( [IGrabbable](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:IGrabbable "Type:IGrabbable"))

The grabbable to check is being grabbed.

## Outputs

### \\* ( [bool](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:Bool "Type:Bool"))

Whether Grabbable ( [IGrabbable](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:IGrabbable "Type:IGrabbable")) is being grabbed or not.
