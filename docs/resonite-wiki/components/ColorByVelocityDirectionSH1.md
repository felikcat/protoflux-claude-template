# Component:ColorByVelocityDirectionSH1

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:ColorByVelocityDirectionSH1

Collapse **Component image**

[![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/thumb/d/dd/ColorByVelocityDirectionSH1Component.png/510px-ColorByVelocityDirectionSH1Component.png)](https://wiki.resonite.com/File:ColorByVelocityDirectionSH1Component.png) **Color By Velocity Direction SH1** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **Color By Velocity Direction SH1** component samples a ColorX spherical harmonic based on the velocity vector of a particle in it's simulation space. that sampled color becomes the particle's color.
