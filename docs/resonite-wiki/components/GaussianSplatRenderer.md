# Component:GaussianSplatRenderer

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:GaussianSplatRenderer

Collapse **Component image**

[File:GaussianSplatRendererComponent.png](https://wiki.resonite.com/index.php?title=Special:Upload&wpDestFile=GaussianSplatRendererComponent.png "File:GaussianSplatRendererComponent.png") **Gaussian Splat Renderer** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **GaussianSplatRenderer** component renders [Gaussian Splats](https://wiki.resonite.com/Gaussian_Splat "Gaussian Splat") into the world.
