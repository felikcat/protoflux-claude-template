# Component:UserAudioStream

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:UserAudioStream

Collapse **Component image**

[File:UserAudioStream\`1Component.png](https://wiki.resonite.com/index.php?title=Special:Upload&wpDestFile=UserAudioStream%601Component.png "File:UserAudioStream`1Component.png") **User Audio Stream\`1** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **UserAudioStream** component writes Audio from a user Audio device to a specified stream. The audio device used is set internally and such methods are not accessible to the user.
