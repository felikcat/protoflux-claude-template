# Component:ColorHSV RangeInitializer

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:ColorHSV_RangeInitializer

Collapse **Component image**

[![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/thumb/d/dd/ColorHSV_RangeInitializerComponent.png/510px-ColorHSV_RangeInitializerComponent.png)](https://wiki.resonite.com/File:ColorHSV_RangeInitializerComponent.png) **Color HSV Range Initializer** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **Color HSV Range Initializer** component initializes a random range of HSV between two different sets for particles.
