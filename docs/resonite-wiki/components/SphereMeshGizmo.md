[![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/thumb/0/01/SphereMeshGizmoComponent.png/510px-SphereMeshGizmoComponent.png)](https://wiki.resonite.com/File:SphereMeshGizmoComponent.png) **Sphere Mesh Gizmo** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **SphereMeshGizmo** component is used to adjust the radius of a sphere mesh.
