[File:LightGizmoComponent.png](https://wiki.resonite.com/index.php?title=Special:Upload&wpDestFile=LightGizmoComponent.png "File:LightGizmoComponent.png") **Light Gizmo** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")
