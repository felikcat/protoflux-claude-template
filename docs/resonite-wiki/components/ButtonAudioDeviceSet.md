The **ButtonAudioDeviceSet** component sets the audio device a [user](https://wiki.resonite.com/User "User") is using, and can only be used in [Userspace](https://wiki.resonite.com/Userspace "Userspace"), as switching audio devices is a [Settings](https://wiki.resonite.com/Settings "Settings") thing.
