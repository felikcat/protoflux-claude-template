[![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/thumb/a/a2/SphereColliderGizmoComponent.png/510px-SphereColliderGizmoComponent.png)](https://wiki.resonite.com/File:SphereColliderGizmoComponent.png) **Sphere Collider Gizmo** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **SphereColliderGizmo** component is used to adjust the radius of a sphere collider.

| `_sphereGizmo` | **[SphereGizmo](https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:SphereGizmo "Component:SphereGizmo")** | The gizmo to use to edit the sphere collider size. |
