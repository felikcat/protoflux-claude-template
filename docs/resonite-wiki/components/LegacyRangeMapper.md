[File:LegacyRangeMapperComponent.png](https://wiki.resonite.com/index.php?title=Special:Upload&wpDestFile=LegacyRangeMapperComponent.png "File:LegacyRangeMapperComponent.png") **Legacy Range Mapper** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **Legacy Range Mapper** component is used in old legacy content from before the [Awwdio](https://wiki.resonite.com/Awwdio "Awwdio") system.
