# Component:ItemShelf

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:ItemShelf

Collapse **Component image**

[![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/thumb/2/22/ItemShelfComponent.png/510px-ItemShelfComponent.png)](https://wiki.resonite.com/File:ItemShelfComponent.png) **Item Shelf** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **Item Shelf** component, often referred to as the tool shelf, is found on the wrists of avatars and is used to place items or tools onto the user's hands for later retrieval or use. It is sometimes used by creations to keep an item on the user's hand.
