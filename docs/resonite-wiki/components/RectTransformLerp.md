The **RectTransformLerp** component takes the [RectTransform](https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:RectTransform "Component:RectTransform") that's on the same [slot](https://wiki.resonite.com/Slot "Slot") and lerps the size & scale of the RectTransform.

| `SourceRect` | **[RectTransform](https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:RectTransform "Component:RectTransform")** | The [RectTransform](https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:RectTransform "Component:RectTransform") to lerp. |
