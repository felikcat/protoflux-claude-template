For detailed information on how this functions for mix and match body parts. Please also see [Equipping Multiple Avatars](https://wiki.resonite.com/Equipping_Multiple_Avatars "Equipping Multiple Avatars").

- This is generated by the [Avatar Creator](https://wiki.resonite.com/Avatar_Creator "Avatar Creator") Dialog
- This handles the click to equip [context menu](https://wiki.resonite.com/Context_menu "Context menu") when clicking on avatars
- Appears to effectively equip to the Root [bodynode](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:BodyNode "Type:BodyNode")
