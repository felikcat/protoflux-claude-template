[![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/thumb/2/29/LocaleAuthorsInfoComponent.png/510px-LocaleAuthorsInfoComponent.png)](https://wiki.resonite.com/File:LocaleAuthorsInfoComponent.png) **Locale Authors Info** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **LocaleAuthorsInfo** component gives the credits for a locale. Usually members of the community that helped with translations.
