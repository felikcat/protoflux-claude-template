_This article or section is a stub. You can help the Resonite wiki by expanding it._

This component is automatically added to [Avatars](https://wiki.resonite.com/Avatar "Avatar") on the Head [BodyNode](https://wiki.resonite.com/Type:BodyNode "Type:BodyNode") to make the first person viewpoint not move around in desktop from the idle head movement generated by the procedural animation system. It will create a [RenderTransformOverride](https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:RenderTransformOverride "Component:RenderTransformOverride") and setup drives for Enabled, PositionOverride, and RotationOverride when attached to a slot.
