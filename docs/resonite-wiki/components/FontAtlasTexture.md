# Component:FontAtlasTexture

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:FontAtlasTexture

Collapse **Component image**

[![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/thumb/5/58/FontAtlasTextureComponent.png/510px-FontAtlasTextureComponent.png)](https://wiki.resonite.com/File:FontAtlasTextureComponent.png) **Font Atlas Texture** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **FontAtlasTexture** component makes a GlyphAtlas containing an atlas of Glyphs representing characters into one large texture that can be used. The internal mechanisms of this component are not assignable directly.
