# Component:ColorByOrientationDirectionSH2

> Source: https://wiki.resonite.com/Component:ColorByOrientationDirectionSH2

Collapse **Component image**

[![](https://wiki.resonite.com/images/thumb/e/ea/ColorByOrientationDirectionSH2Component.png/510px-ColorByOrientationDirectionSH2Component.png)](https://wiki.resonite.com/File:ColorByOrientationDirectionSH2Component.png) **Color By Orientation Direction SH2** component as seen in the [Scene Inspector](https://wiki.resonite.com/Scene_Inspector "Scene Inspector")

The **Color By Orientation Direction SH2** component samples a ColorX spherical harmonic based on the orientation vector of a particle in it's simulation space. that sampled color becomes the particle's color.
